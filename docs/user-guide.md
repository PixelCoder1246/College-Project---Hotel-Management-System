# User Guide

This guide describes how to use the Hotel Management System (v0.1.2) based on different user roles.

## 🔑 Accessing the System

1. Navigate to the landing page.
2. Click **"Get Started"** or **"Sign In"**.
3. If you don't have an account, click **"Create Account"**.
4. After logging in, you may be required to enter a **6-digit OTP** sent to your email.

---

## 🎭 User Roles & Dashboards

The system adapts its interface based on your role.

### 1. Customer Dashboard 🧑
**Focus**: Personal stay management.
- **Room Booking**: Search for available rooms and make reservations.
- **Payments**: View payment history and settle balances.
- **Profile**: Manage your personal information and security settings.

### 2. Staff Dashboard 👷
**Focus**: Hotel operations and guest services.
- **Room Booking**: Manage all guest reservations and check-in/out.
- **Payments**: Process guest payments and view transaction logs.
- **Reports & Analytics**: Access real-time data on hotel performance.

### 3. Admin Dashboard 👑
**Focus**: Full system administration.
- **Staff Management**: Add, remove, or modify employee accounts and roles.
- **Global Reports**: Comprehensive analytics across all hotel branches.
- **Full Access**: Includes all capabilities of Staff and Customers.

---

## 🛠️ Common Tasks

### How to book a room?
1. From the Dashboard, click on the **Room Booking** module.
2. Select your dates and guest count.
3. Choose an available room and confirm.

### How to verify my account?
During registration or login from a new device:
1. Check your registered email for an OTP.
2. Enter the code on the verification screen.
3. If the code is correct, you will be redirected to your dashboard.
